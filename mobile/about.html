About Us

Equinox is the Annual technical fest of the Indian Institute of Information Technology Lucknow. It has been brought into existence with the aim of providing a platform for the Indian student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess in a grand three-day event on the campus of IIIT Lucknow attracting students from all over India.





Welcome To Journey

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. a melting pot of intellectuals and professionals, a launchpad for upcoming technocrats, a spectacular showcase of talent, an epitome of incredible technical experience: where you can never see it all, but what you do acquire lasts a lifetime, where great ideas are born and memories are made amidst the gleam of several lines of code as the nights intensify.

Equinox aims to explore the various aspects of technology and the profound impacts they have on our lives

We hope to constantly grow, evolve, and push the boundaries of what a college fest can be. With something to offer for everyone, Equinox is the ideal destination for all technophiles: young or old, beginner or expert, to witness, learn and experience the wonders of technology..

This year marks the 1st Edition of Equinox, IIIT Lucknow. The fest shall take place from 7th to 9th of February, 2020 at IIIT Lucknow, India. Boasting of a roster of exciting and engaging events, the first edition of Equinox promises to be the grandest, greatest and the most magnificent beginning that a fest could possibly have.

So grab your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for the newest entrant in list of technical festivals..
